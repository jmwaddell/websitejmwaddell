Hello my name is Jonathan Waddell, I am 26 years of age from a town called Northwich based in west Cheshire. So I ended up looking into different career paths, In years to come I had a mixture of Jobs such as labouring for a company called Direct Property Maintenance, I got a mixture of skills from this line of work. I then went on to do a short stay at Morrisons distribution where I was a quality control operative, I had to do various tasks during the day such as checking the quality of fruit and vegetables but also I had an admin roll of sorting excel sheets to display the food safe cleaning agents for each piece of machinery on the production line but the pressure of the job was too much. That is where CodeNation was mentioned to me, and here I am starting my coding journey.
